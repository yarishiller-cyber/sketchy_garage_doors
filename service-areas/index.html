Vancouver

Vancouver's housing is a patchwork — and so are its garage doors.

Burnaby

Burnaby is a city of double-garage family homes — and double-garage homes mean double the springs, cables and rollers that eventually wear out.

Burnaby garage-door repair

Surrey

Surrey is big, fast-growing, and full of newer homes with large double and even triple garages — which is exactly why garage-door repair demand here is huge.

Surrey garage-door repair

Richmond

Richmond sits at sea level on the Fraser delta, and that flat, coastal, salt-laden environment is tough on garage-door hardware.

Richmond garage-door repair

Coquitlam

Coquitlam climbs from the Fraser up into the forested slopes below Burke Mountain, and that range of elevation and tree cover shapes its garage-door wear.

Coquitlam garage-door repair

North Vancouver

On the North Shore, the rainforest climate is the headline.

North Vancouver garage-door repair

Port Coquitlam

Port Coquitlam — PoCo to everyone who lives here — is a tight-knit Tri-Cities community of family homes, and family homes mean garage doors that get hammered every single day.

Port Coquitlam garage-door repair

Port Moody

Port Moody wraps around the head of Burrard Inlet, and that means two things for garage doors: salty inlet air and a lot of newer townhome-and-garage complexes around Suter Brook and Newport Village, plus established homes up toward Heritage Mountain.

Port Moody garage-door repair

New Westminster

New West is the Lower Mainland's oldest city, and it shows in the garage doors: steep streets of heritage character homes above the Fraser, tight rear-lane garages, and a growing wave of new townhomes in Queensborough and along the waterfront.

New Westminster garage-door repair

West Vancouver

West Vancouver's homes climb the North Shore mountains with ocean views — and they tend to be larger, higher-end properties with big, often custom garage doors.

West Vancouver garage-door repair

Delta

Delta spreads across the flat Fraser delta — Ladner, Tsawwassen and North Delta — with big skies, farmland edges and lots of single-family homes with double garages.

Delta garage-door repair

Langley

Langley — both the City and the Township — is where suburbia meets acreage, which means everything from compact townhome garages in Willoughby to big triple-car doors and farm-shop overhead doors out toward Aldergrove and Otter.

Langley garage-door repair

Maple Ridge

Maple Ridge backs onto the mountains and the Golden Ears, with a rugged mix of suburban subdivisions, rural acreages and plenty of detached shops.

Maple Ridge garage-door repair

Pitt Meadows

Pitt Meadows is a flat, friendly farm town between the Pitt and Fraser rivers, framed by the Golden Ears — and its homes range from established neighbourhoods to the newer Bonson builds, plus working acreages with shop doors.

Pitt Meadows garage-door repair

White Rock

White Rock is a seaside town built on a south-facing slope above Semiahmoo Bay — sunny, breezy, and right on the salt water.

White Rock garage-door repair
